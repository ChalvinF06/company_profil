Welcome to PT Green Mine Consultant

PT Green Mine Consultant is a leading provider of mining services, specializing in the preparation of critical documents, including Environmental Impact Analysis (AMDAL), Feasibility Studies, and other necessary permits for mining operations. Our comprehensive services also include exploration solutions designed to support mining companies in achieving their strategic goals.


Founded with the purpose of assisting Indonesian mining companies in implementing good and sustainable mining practices, we recognize the vast mining potential in Indonesia as an opportunity to help mining companies improve their practices and achieve higher standards.


With our expertise and experience, we are well-equipped to support your mining company in achieving better and more sustainable goals.

Company History

Since 2025

The company was founded by three mining engineering graduates with extensive experience in the mining industry. The founders established the company with the goal of becoming a reputable consulting firm that provides valuable services to mining companies by consistently applying Good Mining Practices in accordance with prevailing regulations.
